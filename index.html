It may not go the way you planned,

But what you'll come to understand

Is you can insist on feeling grand.



Let the feelings run their course

(Vulnerability outlasts remorse);

Own every feeling that is yours.



From where you are, can you tell

Whether it is going just as well

As if you came out of your shell?



As many times as it takes,

Remind yourself to stray

The remainder of the day.



Asking why it's that way

Won't help as much today

As finding a place to play.



Though you so expertly blend,

No, you are not here for them;

Behold yourself for you again.



You deserve the gift of space,

The room afforded by grace.

Trust you'll know the place.



Do you feel like you're ready?

Not fully-prepared or steady -

But heart-driven (not heady).



Dare the day to ignore you.

Greet what lies before you.

You're here to explore You.



Who knows the right question -

One that doesn't lessen the lesson,

But that quiets all the guessing?



All the knowledge you've gained,

And all the knowing that remains,

Acknowledges you're who's changed.



Before any upcoming connection,

Trust your powers of perception

To ask the clarifying question.



Though it's taken this long,

You still have your resolve

To rely, then build, upon.



Nothing escapes your calm.

As your awareness sprawls,

You gracefully grasp it all. 



There is no obstacle there,

No formidable foe anywhere

(Busy backbiters, beware).



Clarity isn't necessarily contented.

How else do you need it presented?

Do you prefer it fluid or cemented?



It can also be a whole other way

If you want yourself on display.

What do wish you could convey?



Sometimes you'll land between

Here and where you've just been

(Contrast can still feel serene).



Once your scenery changes,

Something inside rearranges,

Dismantling defenses in stages.



Let it pass. Don't fret.

A thought can stir yet

Still be fit to forget.



You've waited long enough

To be on the receiving end of

Your own custom-tailored love.



Close your eyes and just flow -

Don't strive, or strain, or know -

Just follow where the energy goes.



Since you can't know when

This particular path ends,

Attend to your strengths.



No different than clouds passing,

The feelings won't be outlasting

What your stillness is forecasting.



An inner world that's all your own

Awaits every moment you're alone

(Close your eyes and arrive home).



Rather than work, shift to flow.

Where you are is trying to show

You who in you it's time to grow,



Sometimes, the answer has to be no,

Not for the simple sake of saying so,

But for a better, future yes to grow.



Enough of lingering at the rim.

Where's calling you to dive in,

To soar, spin, swoop, or swim?



You're not quite yet there,

But are certainly somewhere

To handle yourself with care.



It's reflecting back at you -

A sliver of your living proof:

You can't help but breathe truth.



Beyond your innermost brink,

You're stronger than you think

(Experience imitates instinct).



That's behind you. Now, you're here.

Though the turn ahead feels unclear,

You'll straighten it by being sincere.
